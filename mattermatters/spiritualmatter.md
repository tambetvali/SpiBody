# Spiritual Matters

I have seen spirit, in each of it's mystical, "higher-space" or "metaphor", still behaves like material aspectually:
- Telepathy gets stronger by familyhood, brotherhood, felt presence and repeated presence; some spirituality appears with people you see on street.
  - Material connection appears, which can probably explain the material aspect of this sensation, given the models assume that.
- Intent training indeed works if you do intentful activities, where the intent really matters - direct intent is effort needed to move your body.

Thus, altough spiritual view won't see straight down to matter, but rather it's metaphor - even unable to express what is shared in alchemy, flirt, or mystic telepathy; rather the emotions, flows, meanings.

But outside: it can be seen that some kind of material consistency to spirit process might be the material counterpart of spiritual action:
- Personal cognition (mental realm) is seen as projected down into depth by material: our internal process is synchronous to material body.
- Collective consciousness (spiritual realm) is seen as projected up into highs by material: the collective shared throught, it's meaning applied to things. Spirit might not be synchronous to material body, but rather it's integrative position and perspective in it's applied action, felt senses, seek for objectivity.
  - Notice it's like SpiRitual: a ritual is exterior of mind.
- Body projects them both, in it's unity or interaction view - direct cognition of screen of self (Cabbalah calls this screen, altough a little different shade of meaning) or conscious participiation of field.
